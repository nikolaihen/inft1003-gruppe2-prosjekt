Instructions
To play Globethrotter, you need access to a computer. In order to play, you need both a keyboard and a mouse connected to your computer. The game has a shallow learning curve, meaning that anyone at any age can start playing and get good at the game reasonably fast. The goal of the game is to get as high score as you possibly can, and you achieve this by getting as high up as possible without taking too much damage from hitting the platforms or falling to your death.
1 Navigate to "PLAY" on the website. The game will then start.
2 Aim with your mouse to either the left wall or the right wall, and shoot with your left mouse button. This will teleport you to the target you aimed for.
3 If any of the platforms hit you, you will loose one health point. If you loose all of your health, or if you fall down to your death, it's game over.
4 Collect stars to get a combo. This will upgrade your portalgun and increase your score multiplier. You do this by shooting stars with your portalgun.
5 If you hit one of the platforms with your portalgun, your combo will be reset. As you get higher and higher up, the density of the platforms will increase, making the game progressively harder.
